Challenge 5: Accessibility Concerns

I feel that challenges 2-4 all present accessibility issues due to being formated without alts.
Challenge 2, the chessboard is a replica of a board and cant actually function. It also does not have an alt description (and if it did, it would be incredibly complex due to the sheer number of parts and variations of a game). I sugest having certain set ups with certain names preprogramed in. Challenge 3 is inaccessable to those who may have vision issues due to the small font and lack of an alt (maybe an outloud reading option would be nice).
Challenge 4 is inaccessable due to it not having alts either (same sugestion as challenge 3).
